Open Science Database is an experimental project being developed at ISOM Data Analytics Research Lab - Warrington College of Business - University of Florida

We covers every journal with the following two conditions:

  1. The journal has an ISSN
  2. The publisher deposits their meta data with Crossref (list of depositors)
  • If your journal has an ISSN and your publisher is Crossref list but you cannot find your journal in our database please let me know (moqri@ufl.edu)
  • If your journal does not have an ISSN, please request for a free ISSN here
  • If your publisher does not deposit their data with Crossref, I highly recommend asking them to do so: get started

Data Source: Crossref, ImpactStory, and ROAD
